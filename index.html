About Me

Hello there! I'm Mitchel Akoth, a passionate and multi-talented professional specializing in both web development and graphic design. With a love for technology and an eye for aesthetics, I bring a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to every project I work on.

As a web developer, I have honed my skills in various programming languages and frameworks, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. I strive to create engaging and user-friendly websites that not only look stunning but also provide seamless functionality. From crafting responsive layouts to optimizing website performance, I enjoy the challenge of bringing ideas to life in the digital realm.

But my journey doesn't end there. As a graphic designer, I thrive on translating concepts into visually compelling designs. I have a keen understanding of color theory, typography, and composition, which allows me to create captivating graphics for both digital and print media. Whether it's designing logos, brochures, or social media assets, I am committed to delivering visually appealing and impactful designs that resonate with target audiences.
